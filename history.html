History

Power Suction Services was incorporated in 1958 to provide H.V.A.C. systems cleaning and general plant clean-up, making us the longest running, still active H.V.A.C. cleaning company in B.C.

In order to best serve our diversified customer base, our equipment has evolved from the truck mounted fans of yesteryear to the mobile compressors and HEPA filtered negative air systems of today.

As we celebrate our 50th year we can proudly reflect on our commitment to service and the integrity of our work force. We look forward to a bright future servicing the H.V.A.C. cleaning demands of our valued customers for years more to come.

AVALABILITY
