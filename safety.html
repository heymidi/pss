Safety

Since its inception, Power Suction Services has been advocates of continuous and high quality safety training. We constantly invest in training to keep our crew up to date and reminded of the importance of safety during all phases of the projects. We believe that all accidents are preventable and are committed to providing a safe and comfortable work environment for our employees and those around us.

Our Training includes

  • Confined space entry
  • WHMIS
  • First Aid
  • Aerial platform operation
  • Fall arrest
  • Preventive maintenance programs for all equipment
