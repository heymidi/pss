Power Suction Services

Since its conception in 1958 Power Suction Services has been dedicated to providing exceptional HVAC services to the commercial, institutional, marine, and industrial sectors. Our crews have efficiently and safely cleaned HVAC systems in ships, breweries, pulp and paper mills, airports, prisons, hospitals, extended care homes and office towers.

As technology has evolved so too have our practices. Power Suction has grown from truck with mounted fans to the mobile compressors and HEPA filtered negative air systems of today.
